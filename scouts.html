Our Mission

The MNSUAM Scouts aim to develop leadership, discipline, and a spirit of service among students. We strive to create socially responsible individuals who are always ready to help in times of need — both on campus and beyond.

Our scouts actively participate in university events, cleanliness drives, safety patrols, and community outreach programs, embodying the true values of unity and commitment.

Activities & Events

  • ✔️ Campus Cleanliness Campaigns
  • ✔️ Blood Donation Drives
  • ✔️ First Aid & Emergency Response Training
  • ✔️ Organizing University Functions & Events
  • ✔️ Tree Plantation Drives
  • ✔️ Discipline & Crowd Management Support
