Empowering Future Technologists

The Institute of Computing at MNSUAM is committed to producing graduates equipped with modern skills in computing, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and data science. We believe in fostering innovation, creativity, and research-based learning.

Our faculty actively participates in national and international research projects and publishes in renowned journals. We provide students opportunities to work on real-world industry challenges through final year projects and collaborations.

The department regularly hosts workshops, tech talks, and coding competitions to help students stay ahead in the fast-evolving world of technology.
