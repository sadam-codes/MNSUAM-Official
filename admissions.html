How to Apply
  1. Visit the MNSUAM official admissions portal.
  2. Fill out the online application form carefully.
  3. Upload required documents (CNIC, Academic Transcripts, etc.).
  4. Pay the admission processing fee via bank or online transfer.
  5. Submit the application before the deadline.
  6. Check your email regularly for updates and test/interview dates.
